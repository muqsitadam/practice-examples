Scrambled Eggs

by Adam Abdulmuqsit

I am not a fan of egg... I like egg, Weird right. But not everybody is like me "an egg repellant" so I felt like coming up with a recipe for eggs... and behold O you lovers scrambled eggs and here I am

Anyways,Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted addcream and eggs.
  4. Use a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds.
  5. When the eggs are moist remove the heat.
